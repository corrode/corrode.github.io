Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need any prior programming experience?

    For the beginner course you don't need any prior Rust experience. However, you should be familiar with another programming language like Python, JavaScript, Java, C, C++, etc. For the advanced course you should have a good understanding of the Rust basics and be familiar with the Rust ecosystem.

  • How does the live mentorship work?

    Live mentorship involves real-time sessions with experienced Rust developers who guide you through the course material and answer your questions.

  • What if I can't attend the live sessions?

    All live sessions are recorded and made available to students. You can also ask questions in our Discord server.

  • Do I get a certificate?

    Yes, you get a certificate of completion after finishing a course. The certificate mentions the course name, the level, the duration of the course, your name, and the date of completion. You can use the certificate to present to your employer or add to your resume.

  • What if I can't afford the course?

    The courses are open source and available for free. However, we recommend you to enroll in the course to get the most out of it. We offer a limited number of scholarships for students who can't afford the course. Please get in touch.

  • What if I'm not satisfied with the course?

    If you're not satisfied with the course, we'll refund your money. No questions asked.
