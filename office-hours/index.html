Rust in Production - Office Hours

Join us every Thursday at 4 PM UTC for Rust Office Hours.

Note: The Office Hours between our seasons have come to an end. We have some appointments like EuroRust on October 10th (See you there? We’re bringing stickers!, before our 3rd Season begins on October 17th.

What to Expect

We’ll start with a short introduction round , followed by a Q&A session.

If you have any questions about Rust in a production context, this is the place to ask them. We’ll do our best to help you out.

How to Prepare

  • Send questions in advance or ask during the session
  • Include code snippets or error messages if applicable
  • For architecture questions, consider using excalidraw for diagrams

Who Can Join?

Everyone! Newcomers and experienced Rust developers are all welcome.

Participants agree to follow the Rust Community Code of Conduct.

About Office Hours

Our weekly sessions are dedicated to discussing Rust in production environments. We provide an open forum where participants can ask questions, share their experiences, and learn from one another about using Rust in real-world applications. These Office Hours also offer a valuable opportunity to connect with fellow Rust developers, fostering a community of practitioners who are working with or interested in Rust for production systems.
