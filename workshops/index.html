Rust Workshops

My workshops are hands-on: instead of toy snippets, you learn idiomatic Rust by writing real code. Whether that’s building an actual thing (a CLI, a shell, a web app, a test suite) or solving interactive exercises Rustlings-style. Every workshop is free and open-source on GitHub, spans beginner to advanced, and can be booked as a live, tailored session for your team, on-site or remote.
